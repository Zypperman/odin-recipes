Description

Laksa is a spicy noodle soup that sits right at the crossroads of Chinese and Malay cuisines (Peranakan/Nyonya food).

The exact recipe changes from city to cityâ€”and even from stall to stall.

Ingredients

  • Noodles (Usually thick rice noodles or thin rice vermicelli, sometimes both are served together)
  • Curry or Assam Broth
  • Protein & toppings
  • Chilli

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles
  2. throw them into the broth
  3. Add the Protein & toppings
  4. Eat and enjoy
