Description

Pasta is a staple food of traditional Italian cuisine, made from durum wheat flour and water (and sometimes eggs), formed into a vast array of shapes and sizes.

It can be fresh (pasta fresca) or dried (pasta secca) and is typically boiled before serving.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour or “00” flour
  • Eggs
  • A pinch of salt
  • Optional: a drizzle of olive oil

Steps

  1. Knead the dough into noodles
  2. Boil some salted water
  3. Add the noodles in, then cook with toppings
  4. Eat and enjoy
